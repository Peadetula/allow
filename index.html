The problem

You ordered in.
So why are you heading out?

Everything about ordering online is fast and modern, right up until it reaches your gate. Then it stalls in the same four steps, every single time.

What happens now
  1. 01

    The rider arrives with your order and no way to prove they belong at your gate.

  2. 02

    The guard has no information — no name, no unit, no way to verify who they are.

  3. 03

    You walk down, or the rider is turned away, or the order quietly times out.

  4. 04

    Everyone loses — a refund, a cold meal, a rider who skips your building next time.

The insight

The gate is not a security problem.
It is an information problem.

Guards are not the obstacle. They are trained to stop what they cannot verify, and they should. The problem was never their judgment. It was that nobody ever handed them the information to use it. Allow does not replace security. It gives security something worth trusting, so your order gets waved through instead of stuck.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is Allow?

Allow makes sure the food and goods you order online reach your actual door, not just your gate. A rider arrives, we verify them and your order in seconds, and it comes straight up to you.

Do I need a new app?

No. You order the way you already do. Allow works at the gate and keeps you updated by simple message. There's nothing new to learn.

Does this replace my building's guard?

Not at all. The guard still makes every call at the gate. Allow just gives them accurate information so the right delivery gets through quickly.

What does it cost my building?

Nothing. Allow is free for buildings. The verified-entry log and the gate tablet come at no cost to management.

When does it launch?
