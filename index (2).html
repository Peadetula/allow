The problem

You ordered in.
So why are you heading out?

Everything about ordering online is fast and modern, right up until it reaches your gate. Then it stalls in the same four steps, every single time.

What happens now
  1. 01

    The rider arrives with your order and no way to prove they belong at your gate.

  2. 02

    The guard has no information — no name, no unit, no way to verify who they are.

  3. 03

    You walk down, or the rider is turned away, or the order quietly times out.

  4. 04

    Everyone loses — a refund, a cold meal, and a delivery that never should have failed.

The insight

The gate is not a security problem.
It is an information problem.

Guards are not the obstacle. They are trained to stop what they cannot verify, and they should. The problem was never their judgment. It was that nobody ever handed them the information to use it. Allow does not replace security. It gives security something worth trusting, so your order gets permitted through instead of stuck.

FAQ

FAQs

What is Allow?

Allow is a verification layer for deliveries. It confirms who a rider is and that their order is genuine, so your delivery is brought safely, and in trusted hands, all the way to your door instead of being left at the gate.

Does this replace my building's guard?

Not at all. The guard still makes every call at the gate. Allow just gives them accurate information so the right delivery gets through quickly.

What does it cost my building?

Nothing. Allow is free for buildings. The verified-entry log and the gate tablet come at no cost to management.

When does it launch?

We're preparing our first pilot across a small group of Nairobi apartment buildings. Waitlist members hear first as dates are confirmed.
